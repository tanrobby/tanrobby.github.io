FINAL EXAM RULES


This is an important announcement regarding the final exam. Read carefully:
  1. A mock exam is conducted on this Friday (Nov. 20) at 1pm (duration: one hour). This mock exam is MANDATORY. You will not be able allowed to take the actual final exam, if you fail to attend this mock exam.
  2. The mode of the exam is multiple responses. For each question, a full score is given if all responses are correct.
  3. The exam duration is 120 minutes. During this time, you are not allowed to leave without the approval from the invigilator. You must stay all the time even if you have completed your exam.
  4. The exam questions will be randomized and shuffled, and thus the questions and their order are different for each student.
  5. During the exam, only mouse is allowed to use. Keyboard in any forms (including on-screen keyboard) or any other inputs are not allowed.
  6. During the exam, your computer screen must contain only Luminus-Quiz. No other applications are allowed. No internet (other than Luminus-Quiz), pdf, etc to be opened/accessed.
  7. During the exam, you are only allowed to bring/access maximum 2 sheets of A4 papers, where you can write anything you want. No books or other resources are allowed to bring/access.
  8. Invigilation is done through zoom. It means you must use a second device (e.g., your mobile phone) to capture your face, hand, and computer screen, and share it through zoom. See this link for the view arrangement: view arrangement.
  9. You must arrange your second device before logging in to zoom. Otherwise, you will not be allowed to enter the session.
  10. You must log in to the zoom exam session, at least 30 minutes before the exam starts. The zoom session will open 45 minutes before the exam starts.
  11. Student ID must be shown before the exam starts.
  12. Violations on the above rules are considered cheating and will have very serious consequences including possible expulsion from NUS.

FAQs:
  • What happens if there is a permanent internet breakdown that cannot be fixed during the exam?
    You must inform the invigilators immediately when the breakdown happens (by calling the provided emergency number). You must provide evidence that it is the telco's fault in order to be considered for further processes.
  • Is there any place/room in the ECE building where students can sit during the exam? This is because in some cases the internet access at home is unstable.
    If you are from FoE (Zone A), you can email to the Faculty at ug_engin_exam@nus.edu.sg for such a request. If you're not from Zone A, you can contact this email address or ask your respective faculty.
  • What happens if there is a temporary internet breakdown?
    You should contact us immediately (by calling the provided emergency number), and once we can fix the problem, we provide additional time you.
