I am an associate professor at both Yale-NUS College and Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, National University of Singapore (NUS). My main research is in computer vision and deep learning, particularly in the domains of low level vision (bad weather/nighttime, color analysis, physics-based vision, optical flow, etc.), human pose/motion analysis, and applications of deep learning in healthcare.

Previously, I was an assistant professor in Utrecht University. I was also a researcher at Imperial College London, and NICTA/the Australian National University, working with Prof. Richard Hartley. I received my PhD from the University of Tokyo, under the supervision of Prof. Katsushi Ikeuchi.
