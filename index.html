I am associate professor at both Yale-NUS College and Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, National University of Singapore (NUS). My main research is in computer vision and deep learning, particularly in the domains of low level vision (bad weather/nighttime, color analysis, physics-based vision, optical flow, etc.), human pose/motion analysis, and applications of deep learning in healthcare. I received my PhD from The University of Tokyo, and afterward was working at NICTA/Australian National University, Imperial College London, and Utrecht University.
