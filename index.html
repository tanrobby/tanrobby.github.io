I am associate professor at both Yale-NUS College and Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, National University of Singapore (NUS). I also hold a courtesy appointment with School of Computing. My main research is in computer vision and deep learning, particularly in the domains of low level vision (bad weather/nighttime, color analysis, physics-based vision, optical flow, etc.), human pose/motion analysis, and applications of deep learning in healthcare. I received my PhD from The University of Tokyo, and subsequently was affiliated with NICTA/Australian National University, Imperial College London, and Utrecht University.
