I am Chief Scientist at ASUS and also associate professor (on leave) at Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, National University of Singapore. I hold appointments with School of Computing, Institute of Data Science, and NUS Graduate School (NGS/ISEP). My main research is in Artificial Intelligence, particularly in the areas of computer vision, deep learning, and generative AI. I received my PhD from The University of Tokyo, and was affiliated with Australian National University, Imperial College London, and Utrecht University. I regularly serve as the Area Chair of top AI conferences like CVPR, ICCV, NeurIPS, ECCV, among others. I am an editorial board member of IJCV (International Journal of Computer Vision) and associate editor of IEEE TPAMI (Transactions of Pattern Analysis and Machine Intelligence).
