Robby T. Tan

Computer Vision Scientist and Assistant Professor at Yale-NUS College, Singapore



Home » Code » Teaching »

My main research is in computer vision, particularly in the areas of bad weather, physics-based analysis and motion analysis. Besides, I have strong interests in computer graphics and machine learning. Before coming to Singapore, I was an assistant professor in Department of Information and Computing Sciences, Utrecht University, the Netherlands. Prior to that, I was a research associate at Imperial College London, and a research scientist at NICTA/the Australian National University, working with Prof. Richard Hartley. I received my PhD from the University of Tokyo, under the supervision of Prof. Katsushi Ikeuchi.

Email address: robby.tan (att) yale-nus.edu.sg
